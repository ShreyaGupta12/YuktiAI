21 CFR 211.160 – Temperature control must be maintained and documented during drug manufacturing to ensure product stability.

21 CFR 211.113 – Sterilization procedures must be validated and properly recorded to prevent microbial contamination.

WHO GMP Chapter 2 – All manufacturing personnel must be trained, identified, and their activities documented to ensure traceability.

EU GMP Annex 1 – Sterile pharmaceutical products require validated sterilization processes to maintain sterility assurance.

FDA Guidance – Environmental and temperature monitoring must be implemented during production and storage.
